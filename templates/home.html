Laugh. Release. Unite.

We are the fart club at the University of Maryland, College Park! We have a focus on bringing unique people together for collaboration and social events. We aim to push the boundaries of possibility through our unique presence at UMD.

I'm new! What do I do?

The beauty of Fart Club is that you make what you want of it. A couple of things that you can do to get started are:

  • Join the Discord and meet the club members.
  • Get seen on the leaderboard! You can appear on the leaderboard by posting 'fart club' in the #fart-club channel on Discord.
  • Attend events! Our next major event is the Fart Club Formal, to be hosted in Fall of 2023.
